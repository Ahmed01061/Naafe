How jobs work

A quick visual summary of the process on Naafe'.

1. Clients post a job

Clients describe the service they need, and it becomes available for providers like you.

2. Respond with an offer

You see the job request and can choose to respond with a competitive offer for your services.

3. Chat and confirm

Once the client shows interest, you can chat directly to finalize details and confirm the job.

4. Get paid

After you complete the job and the client verifies it, you get paid securely through the platform.

Your phone number is never shared. Chat stays on Naafe'.
