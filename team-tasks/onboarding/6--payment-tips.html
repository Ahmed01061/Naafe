Payment Tips

To ensure secure transactions, always pay your service providers through the Naafe' app. This method offers protection and transparency for both parties. Avoid direct payments outside the app to maintain security and access to support.

How to Pay Securely

1. Add Payment Method

Add your credit/debit card or other supported options to your Naafe' account.

2. Pay Through the App

Once the service is done, initiate the payment to the provider directly from the app.

3. View History

Keep track of all your payments and transactions within the app for easy management.
